RPG Dungeon Starter Guide

Locale Info

Go forth and multi-die.

Here is a list of the potential locations you can send your characters in.

  • Grasslands: No special qualities.
  • Dark Tower: Stats of all player characters and summons with Shadow attacks or resistances are multiplied by 1.1. When at Floor 5 or above, player characters must perform 10% Desperation checks at the beginning of every battle. Desperation lasts one turn.
  • Desert: During the day, 10% chance to Burn player characters and summons at the start of combat, unless they are resistant to either Fire or Burn. During the night, 10% chance to Freeze player characters and summons at the start of combat, unless they are resistant to either Ice or Freeze.
  • Underground: Cannot enter this locale normally. 3% chance to drop into this locale after any battle in most locales.
  • The Void: Every action has a 1% chance to not take effect.
